Turkish Eggs Recipe

Turkish eggs, also known as cilbir, are a quick and easy to make breakfast item. Very healthy option with good fats and protein. Substitute plain greek yogurt with a low-fat yogurt fo a lower calorie option.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain greek yogurt
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tsp Aleppo pepper flakes

Directions

  1. In a small bowl combine room temp yogurt, garlic and a pinch of salt and whisk well.
  2. Bring medium saucepan filled halfway with water to a simmer. Add vinegar.
  3. Poach eggs in water.
  4. Make olive oil sauce in a small pan by warming the oil and mixing in the pepper flakes.
  5. Transfer poached eggs to the bowl of yogurt and drizzle oil on top.
  6. Serve with warm rustic bread.
